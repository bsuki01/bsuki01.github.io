Recommendations

Books

  • The Mind of Napoleon

    An insightful look into the thoughts and strategies of Napoleon Bonaparte.

  • Poor Charlie's Almanack

    Essential wisdom and insights from Charlie Munger.

  • Steve Jobs by Walter Isaacson

    The definitive biography of Apple's visionary co-founder.

  • Elon Musk by Walter Isaacson

    A fascinating portrait of one of today's most influential entrepreneurs.

  • Benjamin Franklin by Walter Isaacson

    The remarkable life of one of America's founding fathers.

  • The Last Lion

    William Manchester's acclaimed biography of Winston Churchill.

  • Surely You're Joking, Mr. Feynman!

    Adventures of a curious character by the Nobel Prize-winning physicist.

  • The Autobiography of Benjamin Franklin

    Franklin's own account of his remarkable life and accomplishments.

Movies & Shows

  • Ghost in the Shell

    Groundbreaking anime exploring identity and consciousness in a cyberpunk future.

  • Memento

    Christopher Nolan's mind-bending thriller about memory and perception.

  • 2001: A Space Odyssey

    Stanley Kubrick's visionary masterpiece on human evolution and technology.

  • Dreams by Akira Kurosawa

    A collection of magical and surreal vignettes from the legendary director.

  • Her

    A poignant exploration of love, connection, and artificial intelligence.

  • Your Name

    Beautiful anime film about connection across time and space.

  • Dune

    Epic sci-fi adaptation of Frank Herbert's classic novel.

  • Jeen-Yuhs

    Intimate documentary chronicling the rise of Kanye West.

  • Attack on Titan

    Dark and intense anime series with complex themes and stunning animation.

  • Naruto & Naruto Shippuden

    Beloved anime following a young ninja's journey to recognition and maturity.
